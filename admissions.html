Admissions 2026

Join the next generation of leaders at Deepa College. Our admissions process is designed to identify talented and motivated students from all backgrounds.

How to Apply

  1. Create an Account: Start by registering on our official online admissions portal.
  2. Fill the Application Form: Provide your personal, academic, and contact details.
  3. Upload Documents: Submit digital copies of your transcripts, test scores, and letters of recommendation.
  4. Pay Application Fee: Complete the application by paying the non-refundable application fee.
  5. Submit & Track: Submit your final application and track its status through the portal.
